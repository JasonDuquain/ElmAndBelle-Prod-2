Meet

Elm & Belle

Welcome to Elm & Belle, a boutique salon studio. Owner and Stylist, Jennifer Duquain LOVES to be behind the chair. Her mission and her work is focused on helping clients discover their inner beauty while having fabulous hair! At Elm & Belle, you can be yourself and leave looking your best. Style and comfort meet to allow for the most personalized salon experience.

With over 15 years of experience, Jennifer specializes in advanced hair color and cutting techniques. Jennifer makes staying current on continuing salon education a priority, offering her clients a combination of experience and the latest trends. She will work with you to select and create your perfect style.

Jennifer has been trained by some of the industry's top stylists and product educators. She has experience with the industry’s best color lines and products from Redken to Pureology to Pravana, Wella, Joico and more. Elm & Belle is also a certified Brazilian Blowout Salon.

Contact Jennifer.
